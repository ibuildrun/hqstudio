# HQ Studio Product Overview

HQ Studio is a premium auto tuning and soundproofing business based in Surgut, Russia. The codebase consists of two applications:

## Web Application (HQStudio site)
A marketing website for the auto tuning studio featuring:
- Service showcase (soundproofing, door closers, ambient lighting, anti-chrome styling)
- Interactive configurator for service packages
- Admin panel for content management
- Contact forms and newsletter subscriptions
- SEO-optimized for Russian market (Yandex, Google)

## Desktop Application (HQStudio exe)
A WPF-based CRM/management system for internal operations:
- Client management
- Order tracking
- Service catalog management
- Staff administration
- Dashboard analytics

## Target Audience
- Russian-speaking car owners seeking premium vehicle customization
- Internal staff managing business operations

## Key Business Features
- Callback request handling
- Service pricing display
- Customer testimonials
- Portfolio/showcase gallery
- FAQ section
